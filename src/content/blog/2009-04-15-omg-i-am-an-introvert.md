Oh My God, after 32 years I have finally realized that I am an Introvert !!

I read this recent post “[Caring for Your Introvert](http://www.theatlantic.com/doc/200303/rauch)” (only cause it appeared on Hacker News) and found so many things familiar, which lead me to question, am I an introvert ?

So as anyone would do I went to Google (the source of all answers) and found many articles and one of them was “[Being an Introvert](http://www.hiddengiftsoftheintrovertedchild.com/being.html)”  and this [self assessment](http://www.hiddengiftsoftheintrovertedchild.com/SelfAssessment.pdf), I got 23 out of 30  so atleast I am not a total Introvert.

I invite everyone to take the above assessment, and figure out what orientation you are.

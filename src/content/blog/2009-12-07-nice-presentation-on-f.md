If you are studying Clojure or for that matter Scala or any other functional programming language I highly recommend that you check out this presentation “[Introduction to Microsoft F#](http://channel9.msdn.com/pdc2008/TL11/)” You can follow along and try out his examples in your own language, the talk is funny with a lots of information that could apply to any functional language. I was really impressed by the F# ide, man I wish Clojure has the same level of IDE support.
